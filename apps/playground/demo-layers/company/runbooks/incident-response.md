# Incident response

## Severity ladder {#severity}

SEV1 pages the on-call immediately. SEV2 opens a channel within 15 minutes. SEV3 is
handled next business day.

## First response {#first-response}

Acknowledge the page, open the incident channel, and post the current impact. Do not
start remediation before the impact is written down.
