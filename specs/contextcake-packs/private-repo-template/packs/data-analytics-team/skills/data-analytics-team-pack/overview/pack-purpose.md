# Pack Purpose {#pack-purpose}

This pack gives a small in-house data and analytics team a shared operating
context for working with an AI agent — the vocabulary, workflows, and
guardrails that keep requests from turning into guesswork.

## What it solves {#what-it-solves}

Most stakeholder requests ("what's our MRR trend?") arrive underspecified.
Without a shared process, an agent either asks too many questions to be
useful or answers too fast and gets the definition wrong. This pack
encodes the four-phase path a request should follow: Clarify, Build,
Validate, Deliver.

## What's inside {#whats-inside}

The full pack includes a glossary of team-specific terms, the hero workflow
and its three sub-workflows, ready-to-use templates for specs, validation,
and insight summaries, a worked example end to end, and policy guidance on
resolving conflicting source-of-truth data.

_The complete version ships with the pack._
