# Source-of-Truth Precedence {#source-of-truth-precedence}

The operational rule behind the glossary entry: when two systems disagree
on the same number, which one wins, and what the agent must do when they
don't.

## What it defines {#what-it-defines}

A precedence order across the systems a Northwind-style team actually
uses — warehouse, CRM, finance system, ad hoc spreadsheets — plus the
required behavior when the top-precedence source is unavailable or looks
wrong.

## Why a policy, not a judgment call {#why-a-policy}

Leaving this to case-by-case judgment produces inconsistent answers to the
same question asked twice. A written precedence rule is what lets the
Validate phase actually validate, instead of just picking whichever number
looks more plausible.
