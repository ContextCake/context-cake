# Insight Summary Template {#insight-summary-template}

The output artifact of the Deliver phase — the structure that turns a
validated result into a stakeholder-ready summary.

## What it captures {#what-it-captures}

The headline finding, a stated confidence level, the caveats that would
change how someone should read the number, and a recommended next
action — not just data, but a point of view on what to do with it.

## Why it's a template instead of ad hoc reporting {#why-a-template}

Consistent structure means stakeholders learn where to look for caveats
and confidence every time, instead of having to hunt for the catch in a
paragraph of prose. It's also what makes insight quality reviewable across
the team over time.
