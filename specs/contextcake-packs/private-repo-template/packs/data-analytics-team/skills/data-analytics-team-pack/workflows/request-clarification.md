# Request Clarification {#request-clarification}

Covers the first phase of the hero workflow: turning "can you pull our
MRR numbers" into a spec precise enough to build against.

## What it walks through {#what-it-walks-through}

A structured pass over the request — metric definition, time window,
segment, and grain — plus rules for when to ask the stakeholder directly
versus when to default to the team's documented conventions. It also
covers when a request is clear enough to skip clarification entirely.

## Why it matters {#why-it-matters}

Most bad analytics answers trace back to a skipped clarification step, not
a bad calculation. A Northwind analyst who assumes "MRR" means the same
thing to Sales and to Finance has already lost the thread. This workflow
is the guardrail against that.
